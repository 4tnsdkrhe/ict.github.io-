What is the website about?

We have a lot of different clubs in our university, and many students don’t even know about their existence. So, here I provide information of these clubs. Thanks to this website, you will learn more about your favorite club and develop your individual hobby skills!

Music Club

Music club is a creative music workshop and our main motto is "If you don't know how, we'll teach you!". It doesn't matter to us how professional you are, we appreciate the love of dancing and music, the desire and desire to learn and improve.

AITU Production

University students from various backgrounds and creative outlets were brought together, and they chose to focus on providing accurate and timely information on student projects and news.

AITU Orchestra

the organization, whose goals include encouraging and promoting national instrument playing among students at Astana IT University. In order to bring all musicians together, form an ensemble, and eventually a whole orchestra, UNITY Orchestra was founded.

Dance club

aimed at bringing together all admirers of dance and promoting the growth of dance in all its forms and directions.

AITUKIT

a group of students with a passion for creating video content. AITUKIT's goal is to document the most captivating and exciting events that take place at Astana IT University in order to demonstrate how cool they are.

AITU PHOENIX

one of the active groups that brought the guys together to plan activities for students' free time and introduce them to amateur art and the creative life. The enthusiastic AITU Phoenix team plans a variety of events for students each semester.

Oratory club

To help them conquer their fear of public speaking, if there is one, of course, I gathered the lads together to practice oratory. The Oratory Club is set up for students who want to advance their knowledge and expertise in public speaking, rhetoric, and public speaking.

Filmy

The club is looking for smart and ambitious students who wish to succeed in the world of film. This is a site that is perfect for you if you are a cameraman, editor, CGI, sound designer, or simply have exceptional charisma and acting abilities!

AITU WEB DESIGN CLUB

It was developed to inspire students at Astana IT University to continue their education in the subject of web development. Students can participate in a number of ongoing club projects, such as our club website and a web application for practical sessions.

SPQR (Senatus Populusque Romanus)

The club based on the Roman Empire. It is dedicated to history, philosophy, politics, economics, as well as all humanities, including the study of foreign languages.

AITU Gaming

The Gaming club was created to expand the opportunities of students interested in esports to take part in various events on the topic of esports.

AITU Mafia

This is a club of fans of the intellectual game "Mafia". The task is simple, civilians must find all opponents and eliminate them by voting in turn.

Chess club

Brought together the guys for whom the popularization of chess is a cultural and intellectual leisure. Chess participants are passionate about this elite game, which was adored by many politicians, scientists, writers, and creative people from different eras.

Reading club

The club periodically gathers students to not only instill in them a love for books but also to discuss the latest book novelties and current issues.

Debate club

The club is a voluntary association that promotes the development of tolerance, public speaking and leadership skills of students.

SCA (What?Where?When?)

The club is a free association of people who are interested in the topics of science and space, various events are held on behalf of the club, ranging from various intellectual games and ending with the invitation of thematic guests, conducting amateur research.

Anime club

This is a club whose goal is to unite AITU students and expand their understanding of Japanese culture.

KCA AITU

The purpose of the student group was to bring together all admirers of South Korean culture. Students' social skills will grow, their horizons will be expanded, and they will learn new things about the education system and the culture of the Land of Morning Freshness thanks to the activities that will be held both within and outside the club.

Volunteers club

Volunteering is a fantastic way for young people, especially students, to gain their first professional experience because it gives them the chance to refine their talents and demonstrate their abilities.

Charity club

This group of kids is prepared to give selflessly to others who are in need. The main goals of the Charity Club are to create a charitable and caring environment at Astana IT University, participate in charitable acts, and promote the habit of giving.

Event club

This group of young, smart, and ambitious individuals decided to inject some fun and activity into AITU's rather dull student life.

Basketball Club

It was developed to promote mass basketball among students and a healthy lifestyle. Basketball instruction is given more focus, which allows pupils to become more physically fit and develop their playing abilities.

Football club

The opportunity for university students who desire to play football is expanding quickly. The growth of mass amateur football is one of the primary responsibilities of football clubs. Football clubs punish their players to make sure they understand that they don't just play "for themselves," but also for the team when they compete in league matches.

AITU Volleyball club

Regularly arranges amicable gatherings and competitions not just with other students but also with professors and university employees. Men's and women's volleyball teams have been created by the AITU Volleyball Club. Every week, students congregate in the hallway to play their favorite game and get away from their schoolwork. Both individuals who already know how to play volleyball and those who want to learn can attend the trainings.
